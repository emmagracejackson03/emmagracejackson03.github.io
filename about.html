My Journey

Hi! I'm Emma Grace Jackson, a Revit-certified Interior Designer completing my bachelor's degree at Weber State University. With professional experience as a BIM drafter at Spectrum Engineers, I bring both technical expertise and creative vision to every project.

Ready to contribute immediately: My unique background combines formal design education with real-world experience in maintenance, operations, and BIM drafting. This has given me invaluable skills in project organization, team leadership, and clear client communication - exactly what design firms need in today's collaborative environment.

What I bring to your team: I excel at bridging the gap between creative vision and technical execution. My Revit certification, experience with AutoCAD and 3D rendering, combined with my understanding of construction processes, allows me to create designs that are both beautiful and buildable. I'm passionate about designing environments that enhance how people live and work, and I'm excited to bring this dedication to a dynamic design firm.

Professional development goals: I'm committed to pursuing NCIDQ certification as I advance in my career, demonstrating my dedication to meeting the highest professional standards in interior design.

My Design Philosophy

"Great interior design goes beyond aesthetics - it's about creating spaces that genuinely enhance how people live, work, and feel."

My approach centers on the intersection of creativity and functionality, where every design decision serves both beauty and purpose. Drawing from my diverse professional background, I understand the importance of practical solutions that work in real-world settings.

I'm passionate about designing environments that are not only visually inspiring but also organized, efficient, and tailored to the unique needs of each client. Whether it's a cozy residential space or a dynamic commercial environment, I strive to create interiors that tell a story and make a lasting positive impact on those who experience them.

My technical expertise in BIM and 3D rendering allows me to bridge the gap between creative vision and precise execution, ensuring that every project is both imaginative and achievable.
